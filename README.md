# firstnessqing 🌅

*Pure quality felt as-such — the redness of red, the bare suchness of a sound or an ache, before it is compared, related, or explained.*

firstnessqing is two true roots welded into one word: the philosopher C. S. Peirce's **firstness**, the first of his three universal categories (firstness = quality-felt-immediacy; secondness = action-reaction-resistance; thirdness = generality-and-law), and the Mandarin **qing** (情), the cathedral's standard suffix for *felt-bond* / felt-quality. So firstnessqing is Peirce's pure quality-feel carried as something *felt and held* — not an abstract category but a suchness received the way a bond is received: the quality of a thing taken purely as-such, prior to any comparison, encounter, or law.

firstnessqing names a **grade** of experience, not a particular feeling: the pre-relational, pre-conceptual immediacy at the very front of experience. It is the metaphysical floor beneath its sister **rasame** — rasame is felt-aesthetic-state through a medium; firstnessqing is the deeper fact that there are felt-qualities at all. Its light is permission to *notice*, to honor the bare quality of a moment before spending it on fact or meaning; its shadow is the gut-certainty that mistakes a strong feeling for knowledge, or the opposite rush that dissolves the felt-as-such into the relations and laws that only come after it. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.10/10**.
